PERFORMANCE

Elevate your driving experience.

Stage 1: ECU Remaps & Intakes

Unlock hidden power without replacing core internals. A robust Stage 1 tune optimizes air-fuel ratios, adjusts ignition timing, and pairs flawlessly with high-flow cold air intakes to deliver an instant 15-20% boost in overall horsepower. Excellent for daily-driven vehicles seeking a sharper throttle response.

Stage 2: Exhaust Systems & Downpipes

De-restrict your engine breathing. By upgrading to decat or high-flow sports cat downpipes coupled with a titanium cat-back exhaust system, backpressure is vastly reduced. This stage unlocks aggressive exhaust notes and paves the way for advanced mid-range torque delivery.

Stage 3: Suspension Dynamics

Power requires control. Upgrading to fully adjustable coilovers or pneumatic air suspension systems drastically drops the center of gravity, dials out massive body roll, and optimizes track camber geometries. Essential for canyon carving and rigorous track days.

Stage 4: Carbon Ceramic Brakes (CCB)

Shed unsprung mass to conquer brake fade completely. Multi-piston slotted or carbon-ceramic big brake kits dramatically shorten stopping distances even under immense thermal stress gradients from continuous 150mph to 0 braking cycles.

Stage 5: High-Downforce Aerodynamics

Bend the air to your absolute will. Implementing dry carbon splitters, specialized canards, flat underfloors, and active swan-neck rear wings mechanically forces the chassis into the tarmac, allowing staggering high-speed cornering stability.
